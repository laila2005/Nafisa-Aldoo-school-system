# Subjects Offered at Nafisa Aldoo School

The Nafisa Aldoo School offers a diverse curriculum designed to provide students with a comprehensive education. Below is a list of subjects available, along with their descriptions and codes.

## List of Subjects

1. **Arabic Language - اللغة العربية**
   - **Code:** AR-101
   - **Description:** Teaching the Arabic language and literature, focusing on reading, writing, and comprehension skills.

2. **Mathematics - الرياضيات**
   - **Code:** MATH-101
   - **Description:** Instruction in mathematical concepts, including arithmetic, geometry, and basic algebra.

3. **Islamic Education - التربية الإسلامية**
   - **Code:** ISLAM-101
   - **Description:** Education on Islamic teachings, principles, and the Quran, fostering moral and ethical development.

4. **English Language - اللغة الإنجليزية**
   - **Code:** ENG-101
   - **Description:** Teaching the English language, emphasizing communication skills, grammar, and vocabulary.

5. **Geography - الجغرافيا**
   - **Code:** GEO-101
   - **Description:** Study of geographical concepts, including physical geography, human geography, and environmental issues.

6. **Technology - التكنولوجيا**
   - **Code:** TECH-101
   - **Description:** Introduction to technology and computer science, covering basic computer skills and digital literacy.

7. **History - التاريخ**
   - **Code:** HIST-101
   - **Description:** Exploration of historical events, figures, and movements, promoting an understanding of the past.

8. **Science - العلوم**
   - **Code:** SCI-101
   - **Description:** Instruction in natural sciences, including biology, chemistry, and physics, encouraging scientific inquiry and experimentation.

## Conclusion

The subjects offered at Nafisa Aldoo School are designed to equip students with essential knowledge and skills, preparing them for future academic and personal success. Each subject is taught by qualified educators who are dedicated to fostering a positive learning environment.
