# User Roles in Nafisa Aldoo School Management System

The Nafisa Aldoo School Management System defines several user roles, each with specific permissions and responsibilities to ensure smooth operation and management of the school. Below is a detailed description of each role:

## 1. Administrator (ADMIN)
- **Responsibilities**: 
  - Oversee the overall management of the school system.
  - Manage user accounts, including teachers, students, and staff.
  - Handle administrative tasks such as enrollment, class assignments, and academic year management.
  - Generate reports and statistics related to school performance.
  
- **Permissions**:
  - Access to all system features and settings.
  - Ability to create, read, update, and delete records across all modules.
  - Manage academic years, class sections, and subjects.

## 2. Teacher (TEACHER)
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Deliver educational content and manage classroom activities.
  - Assess student performance and provide feedback.
  - Communicate with students and parents regarding academic progress.
  - Participate in curriculum development and school events.
  
- **Permissions**:
  - Access to student records within their classes.
  - Ability to enter grades and attendance.
  - Limited access to administrative functions related to their subjects.

## 3. Student (STUDENT)
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Attend classes and participate in school activities.
  - Complete assignments and assessments as directed by teachers.
  - Maintain communication with teachers regarding academic progress.
  
- **Permissions**:
  - Access to personal academic records and schedules.
  - Ability to view grades and feedback from teachers.
  - Limited access to resources and materials provided by the school.

## 4. Staff (ADMIN)
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Support administrative functions such as registration and accounts.
  - Maintain cleanliness and safety of the school environment.
  - Assist in various operational tasks as needed.
  
- **Permissions**:
  - Access to specific administrative functions relevant to their roles.
  - Ability to manage non-academic records and assist in student services.

## Summary
The user roles within the Nafisa Aldoo School Management System are designed to facilitate effective management and operation of the school. Each role has defined responsibilities and permissions to ensure that users can perform their tasks efficiently while maintaining the integrity and security of the system.
